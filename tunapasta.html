Pasta with tuna

A meal fit for the gods of the sea

This godlike meal satisfies every appetite and quenches every hunger.

Mix of pasta, tuna, cheese and cream make this dish something to wish for.

Ingredients

  • 400g of spaghetti
  • teaspoon of salt
  • 2 cans of Rio mare tuna
  • 50g of butter
  • 200 g of cooking cream
  • 50g of parmiggiano

Instructions

  1. Get a pot, fill it with water and set it to boil
  2. Add a teaspoon of salt into the water
  3. When the water is boiling, add the spaghetti into the pot and cook for 7-10 minutes
  4. When the spaghetti are cooked, put them in a separate pot. Mix butter and tuna into the first pot while its still hot
  5. Add cooking cream and parmiggiano.
  6. Serve the dish and enjoy

Bon appetit
